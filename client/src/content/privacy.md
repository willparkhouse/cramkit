# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 7 April 2026_

This policy explains what data cramkit collects, why, and what we do with it.

cramkit is operated by **William Parkhouse**, a sole trader based in the United Kingdom. The data controller is William Parkhouse. You can contact us at **wjdparkhouse@gmail.com**.

---

## What we collect

When you create an account:

- Your **email address**, used for authentication and account-related notifications.
- A **user ID** generated by Supabase (our auth provider).

When you use the app:

- Your **module enrollments**, **quiz history**, **knowledge scores**, and **revision schedule** — needed to make the app work.
- Your **AI provider API key** if you use Bring Your Own Key. This is stored in your browser's local storage only. **It is never sent to our servers.**

If you subscribe to the managed plan:

- **Payment details** (handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card number; we only receive a customer ID and subscription status).

We do **not** collect:

- Tracking cookies or analytics across other sites
- Browser fingerprints
- Location data
- Any data we don't need to make the service work

## Where data is stored

- **Account, quiz, and revision data** are stored in Supabase, in the EU (Frankfurt) region.
- **Your BYOK API key** never leaves your own browser.
- **Payment data** is stored by Stripe under their own terms.
- **Lecture transcripts and slide content** are stored alongside your account data in Supabase EU. These are not personal data, but they are searchable on your behalf.

## Third parties we share data with

We only share data with the providers needed to make cramkit work:

| Provider | What they receive | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** (EU) | Account, quiz, and revision data | Database + auth |
| **Stripe** | Email, payment details | Subscription billing |
| **Anthropic** (Claude API) | Your AI queries and the course material we send as context | Generating quiz questions, answer evaluation, the tutor chat |
| **OpenAI** (Embeddings API) | The text of your search queries when using "Search materials" or the wrong-answer panel | Computing embeddings for relevance ranking |

When you use **BYOK**, calls to Anthropic are made directly from your browser using your own API key. We do not proxy them or see them.

When you use the **managed plan**, calls to Anthropic are made from our server using our key. The content of your queries is processed by Anthropic in line with their commercial terms; Anthropic does not use API inputs to train its models.

OpenAI is used only for embedding short text queries (we do not send your full quiz history or personal data). OpenAI also does not train on API inputs by default.

## How long we keep data

- **Account data**: as long as your account is open.
- **Quiz history and knowledge scores**: as long as your account is open, so the spaced-repetition scheduler works.
- **Payment records**: at least 6 years after the relevant tax year, to comply with UK record-keeping requirements.
- **Lecture transcripts and slide content**: indefinitely, because they are not personal data.

When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

## Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** any inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to erasure")
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Object** to processing in certain circumstances
- **Withdraw consent** where we rely on consent (you can do this at any time by deleting your account)

To exercise any of these rights, email **wjdparkhouse@gmail.com** from the address on your account. We'll respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you think we've handled your data unlawfully: [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk).

## Cookies

cramkit uses a small number of strictly necessary cookies for authentication (Supabase session) and to remember your interface preferences. We do not use any analytics or advertising cookies.

## Children

cramkit is intended for university students aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we'll delete it.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that materially affects how we handle your data, we'll notify you by email or in the app at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
